Gamitorial

Mobile devices are nowadays “Swiss Army Knives”. They support a wide range of tasks, enabling people access a wealth of information and services through their extensive connectivity; they have the potential to empower people in everyday tasks. Unfortunately, getting started with a smartphone is not an easy task, particularly for blind users. The tutorials and help systems that come out of the box are not enough for a user to even get started. This masters is aimed to address the problems people face when first interacting with a new mobile device by gamifying in context tutorials for users to learn everything from gestures to tasks.

Possible Goals: *Gamified tutorials for gestures *Gamified tutorials for tasks *Explore different gamification approaches: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dBmIkEvEBtA (4 Step level design) *When and how to prompt tutorials?
